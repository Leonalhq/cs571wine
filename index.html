Wine Visulization


Best Wine Review Visulization for Both Costomer & Merchandise
Umass Amherst Spring 2021

We dedicted to present more illstraued visulized graph for wine buyer as well as producer. Here are the graphes we think could be helpful for them to understand the trends in wine market. Hope all of you can enjoy :)


Map

Map is good for presenting information in geographic and comprehensive way. User can switch from point, price and production view, and they can zoom in and out to find sepefic country details.When user move their cursor, the tooltip gives detailed information such as std and top winery list. In this way, user can easily discover regions with the highest/lowest wine ratings and analyze geographic influence on different wine qualities, prices, and points.


Bar Chart

Bar chart is good for presenting information and making comparisons, it perfectly intergrated with most of our comparisons. We can Find out what kinds of flavor or key component consumers like most to investigate on opponent’s products and develop their own.
Discover wines with the highest/lowest ratings.
Discover the top-10 wines that have the highest/lowest prices.
Discover the top-10 countries that produce the most amount of wines, and make a comparison of the number of wines.
Analyze a wine dataset and discover the correlation between popularity and the top 100 wine category
Discover the distribution of the number of reviews for each taster
Analyze the price-performance ratio of each product based on their price and rating


Scatter Plot

Scatterplot is good for presenting information and making comparisons. User can found the corrlation bettween prices, popularity, and points by simply click the bar.
For price VS popularity, user is given the option to scale the range since most of the price fall in to $5 to $75


Word Cloud

Word cloud give both buyer and marchndisers view of popular wine's property over the world. We label the category mannually by fruit:purple spice:orange floral:pink sherry:yellow microbiological:red pungent:green
In the graph we can see two slider, one is for wine and the other one is for it's review's range which user could interact with.
